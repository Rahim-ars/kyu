KYU Register Child Safety Standards

KYU Register is an attendance management application designed exclusively for Kyambogo University students and staff. We have a zero‑tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This page fulfills Google Play's requirement for published safety standards.

1. Zero‑Tolerance for CSAE

We strictly prohibit any content or activity that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form (images, videos, text, or links).
  • Grooming, sextortion, or trafficking of minors.
  • Any content that sexualizes children or promotes inappropriate interactions with minors.

Any user found violating this policy will have their account permanently banned and may be reported to law enforcement and the Uganda Police Force Child Protection Unit.

2. Age Restriction

KYU Register is intended solely for university‑aged students (typically 18+). We do not knowingly allow users under the age of 13. If we learn that a user under 13 has created an account, we will immediately delete the account and all associated data.

3. Reporting CSAE and CSAM

We provide the following channels to report suspected child safety violations:

  • In‑App Reporting: Users can report any suspicious behavior via the App's feedback feature.
  • Direct Email: For urgent child safety matters, contact our designated point of contact below. We aim to acknowledge all reports within 24 hours.

4. How We Handle CSAM Reports

When we receive a report of potential CSAM, we follow this strict process:

  • Immediate Review: Our safety team examines the reported content within hours.
  • Removal & Ban: If content is confirmed as CSAM, we immediately remove it and permanently suspend the offending account.
  • Reporting to Authorities: As required by law, we report all apparent CSAM to the Uganda Police Force and cooperate fully with investigations.
  • Preservation: We preserve relevant account data and content for legal purposes.

5. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

KYU Register complies with all applicable child protection laws, including the Uganda Children Act and international standards. We regularly review our policies to align with evolving legal requirements.
